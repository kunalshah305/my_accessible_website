GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL MECHANISM
(for Accessibility Issues)

In compliance with the SEBI circular dated 31 July 2025, Kunal Hasmukhlal Shah has established a dedicated grievance redressal mechanism to address accessibility-related complaints from persons with disabilities (PwDs).

Dedicated Channels

  • Email: support@finsightsbykunal.com
  • Helpline: +91-9920185889 (operational Mon–Fri, 9:30 AM – 6:00 PM)
  • Web Form: Available Below

Process

  • All accessibility-related grievances will be acknowledged within 2 working days.
  • Resolution/response will be provided within 15 working days.
  • Complex issues requiring longer timelines will be communicated clearly to the complainant.
