REFUND POLICY

At FinsightsByKunal, we value our customers and are committed to providing the best possible services. While we strive to deliver a high level of satisfaction, we understand that situations may arise where a client may wish to discontinue their subscription.

In such cases, refunds will be processed on a pro-rata basis for the unutilized portion of the subscription, subject to the following terms:

  • A written request for cancellation and refund must be submitted to our support team.
  • The refund amount will be calculated based on the remaining subscription period, after deducting charges for the services already utilized.
  • Refunds will be processed within 7 business days of receiving and verifying the request.
  • Clients are encouraged to carefully review our service details, Terms & Conditions, and Privacy Policy before making payment.

We remain committed to supporting our clients and ensuring their experience with us is positive and transparent.
